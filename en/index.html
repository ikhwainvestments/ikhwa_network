About Our Company

Ikhwa Network is a Moroccan real estate company operating domestically and internationally. We leverage our years of accumulated expertise in property development to deliver premium services to our clients. Our board of directors continuously works to enhance transaction structures and strengthen relationships with strategic field partners, ensuring the highest levels of craftsmanship, quality, and commitment while leaving our distinctive mark on every project we undertake.

Frequently Asked Questions

We've compiled answers to the most common questions about our company.

What is Ikhwa Network?

Ikhwa Network is a real estate investment company operating in Meknes, the Ismaili capital, established in 2023.

What is the company's legal structure and name?

The company is a multi-partner limited liability company registered at the Meknes Commercial Court under number 59287, with the official legal name IKHWA NETWORK SARL.

Who legally represents Ikhwa Network?

The legal representative is Mr. Mohamed Saad Raji, authorized by the board of directors.

Where is Ikhwa Network located?

Ikhwa Network is headquartered in Meknes at Villa 194, Riyad Toulal No. 1.
