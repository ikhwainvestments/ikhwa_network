ARTYADS LLC

Established in early 2018 in the Ismailia capital Meknes, ARTYADS is the leading online network for painters and photographers with members from multiple countries. Artists come to the platform to showcase their distinctive artworks and promote them to art enthusiasts. The company organizes art exhibitions and events both within Morocco and internationally. Additionally, ARTYADS provides website development, digital marketing, and social media management services for artists, along with professional business management representation both domestically and abroad.

IKHWA INVESTMENTS BY IKHWA NETWORK LLC

Established in early 2024 and headquartered in Meknes, Morocco, Ikhwa Investments benefits from decades of collective expertise among its staff.
